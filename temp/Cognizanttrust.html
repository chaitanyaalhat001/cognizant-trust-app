For 20 years, Cognizant Foundation has been a lifeline for underserved communities, restoring vision, delivering critical healthcare, and extending support to those in need. At the same time, it has empowered students with advanced learning tools, nurtured exceptional talent, and opened doors to opportunities in technology. These efforts have transformed countless lives and bridged gaps in education, healthcare, and livelihoods. This milestone celebrates an unwavering commitment to creating a more inclusive and equitable world. Here’s to:

#TwoDecadesOfEmpowerment

 
At Cognizant Foundation India, we leverage technology and collaboration for greater impact and aim to promote a culture of inclusion.

Our commitment is to ensure a better future for all by enabling participation and access to resources and opportunities for the marginalized sections of the society. 

Our programs are centered around 3 aspects: empowering persons with disabilities, promoting holistic child development, and fostering gender equality. Our aim is Elevating communities towards a brighter tomorrow!

Areas of work

Health4All

Enabling accessibility to quality healthcare

Our core focus is to support timely eye care through Sight4All program, promote maternal and child health through Care4All program and provide comprehensive early intervention and rehabilitation services to children with special needs through Support4All program. 

Read more

Future4All

Creating a better tomorrow through education and skilling

Our core focus is to empower underserved sections through STEAM based education and supporting educators in enhancing teaching capabilities through STEAM4All program, accelerate access to higher education through scholarships through Excellence4All program and provide tech-oriented skilling programs for women and persons with disabilities through Tech4All program.
